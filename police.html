About Our Police Station

KPHB Police Station is a well-staffed, modern facility under Cyberabad jurisdiction, playing a vital role in law enforcement, traffic management, and crime response in Kukatpally. It offers strong accessibility, emergency services, and improved infrastructure, though public opinion varies due to occasional operational issues. The team remains active in major operations and community policing.

Key Focus Areas

Cyber Crimes

Cyber crimes in KPHB have been rising. As part of Cyberabad Police, we actively track and act on cases of phishing, fraud, and online harassment through a dedicated cyber cell.

Women Safety

Special patrol teams and SHE Teams are deployed to ensure the safety of women in public spaces and to take quick action on any complaints received.

Traffic Regulation

Our traffic wing ensures smooth flow during peak hours, prevents illegal parking, and promotes road safety awareness.
